About Us

Welcome to The Maharishi School of Excellence Senior Secondary

A Campus with Nursery to Post Graduate Level Education Maharishi School of Excellence Senior Secondary, Chennai is a part of Maharishi Global Education Movement. In India Maharishi Vidya Mandir School chain is one of the largest school systems with with 150 branches in 14 states. About 6500 teaching, administrative and support staff are busy training as many as 85000 students from KG to 12th standard. The school is running under the Society MAHARISHI SHIKSHA SANSTHAN and the Registration is valid upto 99 years.
Maharishi School of Excellence Senior Secondary, Chennai has been established in the year 2010 and is situated at P.T.C. Colony area of Chennai in 4.54 acres of land with vast infrastructure, state of the art facilities including audio visual teaching system, computers, library, reading room, music room, art room, transcendental meditation hall, yoga hall etc.
